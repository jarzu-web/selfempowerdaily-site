Frequently Asked Questions

How does the voice cloning work?

You record answers to four short questions. Our AI technology analyzes your voice patterns and creates a digital voice that sounds like you. This voice is then used to speak your personalized daily affirmations.

Is my voice data private?

Yes. Your voice recordings are stored on your device and are only used to create your voice clone. They are never stored on our servers or shared with advertisers. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Can I change my affirmation goals?

Yes. Tap the settings icon on the home screen and select "Update My Goals." You'll re-record your answers and receive fresh affirmations. Your streak and plant growth will stay.

How do I change my morning reminder time?

Tap the clock badge at the top of your home screen. A time picker will appear where you can set a new reminder time.

What happens if I miss a day?

Your streak will reset, but your plant growth stage is preserved. Just press play the next morning and start building your streak again.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions. Find Self Empower Daily and tap Cancel Subscription. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Open the app and tap "Restore Purchases" on the subscription screen. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.
